About

The WUHSMS Endowment Association allows students to request for grants and has helped many people achieve goals that benefit our school and community.

Financial Goals of The Association

The long range plan of the Association is to develop an endowment sufficient to fund all approved grants. Should an innovative program be submitted foe which there are insufficient funds, the Trustees are empowered to use a portion of the endowmentâ€™s unrestricted principal.

Proposal Development and Writing

The WUHSMS Endowment Association has found it helpful to consult with the grant applicants prior to submission of a proposal. Applicants are encouraged to contact a trustee or their principal before beginning the application process.
