Deadlines and Notification

Grant proposals will be considered twice a year. Application deadlines are:

  • October 15
  • March 15

Grant awards are made approximately 45 days after the application deadlines.

Proposal Development

The WUHS Endowment Association has found it helpful to consult with the grant applicants prior to submission of a proposal. Applicants are encouraged to contact a trustee or their principal before beginning the application process.

Submit a Proposal

Grant applicants may choose whether to submit their proposals online or by mail. Please note that online submissions require a Google account.

How to Submit Online

  1. Click here to copy the Application Form into your Google account. (Don't have a Google account? Please follow the steps below for "How to Submit By Mail".)
  2. Change the Share settings to "Anyone with the link can view".
  3. Complete all the green sections in the application. Return to the document at any time by searching for it in your Google Drive.
  4. Print the Signature Page, and have it signed by your building principal.
  5. Scan the signed Signature Page and save it to in your Google Drive.
  6. Change the Share settings of the Signature Page to "Anyone with the link can view".
  7. Go to our Online Document Submission Form to submit your application documents to the Endowment Association.
  8. After reviewing your documents, a member of the Board of Trustees of the Endowment Association will be in contact with you about your application.

How to Submit By Mail

  1. Print the Application Form and complete it.
  2. Print the Signature Page, and have it signed by your building principal.
  3. Make copies of both documents to keep for your records.
  4. Mail both documents to the Endowment Assocation at: WUHS Endowment Association, P.O. Box 118, Woodstock, VT 05091
  5. After reviewing your documents, a member of the Board of Trustees of the Endowment Association will be in contact with you about your application.
